Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake topped with creamy frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl, wet in another.
  3. Combine both mixtures, pour into a baking tin.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Let cool and frost.

Homemade Pizza

Easy homemade pizza dough with classic toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Tomato sauce, cheese, toppings

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add water and olive oil. Knead into dough.
  3. Let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out dough, add sauce and toppings.
  5. Bake at 220°C (430°F) for 15-20 minutes.

Apple Pie

Traditional apple pie with a flaky crust and sweet filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4-5 apples (sliced)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Make crust with flour and butter, chill.
  3. Mix apples with sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Fill crust with apples, cover with top crust.
  5. Brush with egg wash. Bake for 45-50 minutes.
